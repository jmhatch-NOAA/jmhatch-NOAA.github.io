Analysis & Applications to Improve Protected Species Assessments in the Greater Atlantic Region


In collaboration with:

By working together with governmental and research partners, the Sea Turtle Ecology & Population Dynamics group of the PSB (Protected Species Branch), NEFSC (Northeast Fisheries Science Center), NMFS (National Marine Fisheries Service) proposes to develop a set of analysis and web applications that will improve protected species assessments in the Greater Atlantic Region (Maine through Cape Hatteras, North Carolina). This information will aid the Greater Atlantic Regional Fisheries Office (GARFO) Sea Turtle Program, which works to conserve and rebuild populations of sea turtles in U.S. Northeast and mid-Atlantic waters.
